- Build emotional connection through storytelling

**Creative Focus:** Teaches **narrative design and emotional engagement** - making apps feel like stories.

**Creative Focus:** **Multisensory creative expression** through audio design.

#### 4. **Creative Problem-Solving & Ideation** (MEDIUM PRIORITY)

**Creative Focus:** **Creative thinking methods and innovation techniques**.

**Creative Focus:** **Attention to detail, personality in design, user joy**.

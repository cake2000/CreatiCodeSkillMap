# T10 (Lists & Tables) Optimization - Complete Deliverables Index

## Project Overview

Complete optimization of Topic T10 (Lists & Tables) skills, breaking down overly broad skills into focused, single-block skills for better learning outcomes.

**Result:** 96 skills → 112 skills (+16 skills, +17%)

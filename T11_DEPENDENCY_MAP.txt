===============================================================================
KEY OBSERVATION
===============================================================================

The T11 progression shows excellent pedagogical design:
- Starts conceptual (understanding WHEN and WHY)
- Moves to basic implementation (HOW to create)
- Expands to parameters (making blocks flexible)
- Focuses on design principles (making blocks WELL)
- Advances to CS concepts (encapsulation, modularity)
- Culminates in expert application (libraries, trade-offs)

Grade 6 skills (G6.01, G6.03, G6.04) serve as crucial bridge skills between
implementation (G3-G5) and advanced application (G7-G8).

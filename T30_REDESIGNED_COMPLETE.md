# T30 – Internet & Cloud: Complete Redesigned Skill List

## Summary
This redesign reduces the total from 100 to 94 skills by:
- Consolidating repetitive K-2 sorting activities
- Adding smooth conceptual transitions in G3-G4
- Scaffolding multiplayer introduction in G4-G5
- Consolidating block-specific skills in G6-G8 into conceptual skills
- Adding AI-as-cloud-service skills (G5-G8)
- Strengthening debugging and design skills throughout

---

## KINDERGARTEN (4 skills)

### ID: T30.GK.01
Topic: T30 – Internet & Cloud: Kindergarten
Skill: Sort devices by internet connection (picture-based)
Description: Students drag picture cards of devices (tablet with video call, laptop with web browser, smart speaker, game console, alarm clock, flashlight, toy car) into two bins: "Needs Internet" and "Works Without Internet." Audio narration helps non-readers understand what each device does.
CSTA: EK-SAS-NW-02
Dependencies: None

### ID: T30.GK.02
Topic: T30 – Internet & Cloud: Kindergarten
Skill: Match activities to their devices (picture-based)
Description: Students see pictures of internet activities (video calling grandma, watching videos, listening to music online, playing games with friends) and drag them to match the correct devices. They learn that different devices can do the same internet activities.
CSTA: EK-SAS-NW-02
Dependencies: T30.GK.01

### ID: T30.GK.03
Topic: T30 – Internet & Cloud: Kindergarten
Skill: Recognize loading and waiting signs (picture-based)
Description: Students identify visual indicators showing the internet is working (spinning circles, loading bars, hourglass icons, buffering symbols). They match these icons to activities like watching videos or loading games, understanding that waiting means "the internet is getting something for you."
CSTA: EK-SAS-NW-02
Dependencies: T30.GK.01

### ID: T30.GK.04
Topic: T30 – Internet & Cloud: Kindergarten
Skill: Sort content by source: internet vs device (picture-based)
Description: Students view scenarios showing content from the internet (streaming a song, watching a video, seeing a website) vs content on the device (photos in gallery, offline drawing, saved games). They sort picture cards into "Internet Brings It" and "Already On My Device" categories.
CSTA: EK-SAS-NW-02
Dependencies: T30.GK.01

## GRADE 4 (7 skills)

Description: Students learn that data is broken into packets (small pieces with addresses and sequence numbers), sent separately across the internet through different paths, and reassembled at the destination. They simulate this by splitting a message into numbered pieces, routing them through different network paths on a diagram, then reassembling in order.

### ID: T30.G4.04
Topic: T30 – Internet & Cloud: Grade 4

### ID: T30.G4.05

Description: Students analyze scenarios with connection failures (wrong Wi-Fi network, weak signal bars, mistyped URL, server maintenance message, firewall blocking) and identify the issue from clues (error messages, symptoms, context). ### ID: T30.G4.06

Skill: Predict and explain slow connection behavior
Description: Students predict what happens with slow connections (videos buffer frequently, pages load images slowly, games lag/stutter, downloads take long time) vs lost connections (ongoing actions fail immediately, cached content still accessible, reconnection attempts start). They create and join a cloud session, observe how changes made by one user appear for others, and explain how the cloud server stores and syncs the shared data.

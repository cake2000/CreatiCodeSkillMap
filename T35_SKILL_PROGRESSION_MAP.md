## New Skills Strategic Placement

### Why G3.04? (Data Collection Recognition)
**Gap Filled:** Between G3.01 (footprints) and G6.02 (privacy)
**Rationale:** Students need to understand WHAT data is collected before analyzing WHY/WHETHER it's justified
**Features:** Widgets + variables (age-appropriate)

### Why G5.04? (Stakeholder Visualization)
**Gap Filled:** Between G5.03 (AI impacts analysis) and data ethics
**Rationale:** Students analyze differential impacts but need data literacy to visualize inequities
**Features:** Google Sheets + widgets + surveys (mid-level complexity)

### Why G6.05? (Consent Mechanisms)
**Gap Filled:** Between G6.02 (privacy analysis) and G7.04 (surveillance)
**Rationale:** Understanding privacy tradeoffs should lead to practicing consent implementation
**Features:** Widgets + cloud storage + conditionals (hands-on GDPR concepts)

### Why G7.07? (Visualization Ethics)
**Gap Filled:** Between G7.01 (bias audits) and G8.03 (impact assessment)
**Rationale:** Data literacy includes recognizing misleading visualizations
**Features:** Tables + widgets + graphics (critical thinking through design)

### 1. Empirically-Driven Gateway Skills

Not all skills are equal. The 6 gateway skills were identified based on:
- Dependency analysis (how many skills unlock)
- Cognitive importance (foundational concepts)
- Research evidence (critical for progression)

**Result:** Teachers know where to focus effort for maximum impact.

### 2. IXL-Quality Specifications

Each skill follows proven IXL model:
- One clear learning objective
- Concrete, auto-gradable outcome
- 15-30 minute completion time
- Immediate feedback
- Scaffolded progression

**Result:** Proven engagement and mastery model.

### 3. Complete Teaching Support

Not just "what to teach" but "how to teach it":
- Step-by-step instructions
- Common errors anticipated
- Hints and solutions provided
- Sample lesson plans
- Assessment rubrics

**Result:** Teachers can implement immediately, no prep needed.

### 4. Auto-Grading Ready

Every skill includes:
- Structure checks (block presence, connections)
- Runtime checks (behavior, outcomes)
- Clear pass/fail criteria

**Result:** Scalable assessment, immediate feedback.

### 5. Differentiation Built-In

Strategies for:
- Struggling students (break down further)
- Advanced students (extension challenges)
- ELL students (visual supports)
- Special needs (multiple modalities)

**Result:** All students can succeed.

**Result:** Students ready for independent coding.

- Extra teaching time allocated
- Multiple practice activities
- Small group intervention

**Likelihood:** Medium
**Impact:** High (disrupts learning)
**Mitigation:**

- Backup activities (unplugged)

**Impact:** Medium (quality varies)

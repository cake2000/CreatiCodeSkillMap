## Executive Summary

The CreatiCode K-2 Skill Map now contains **206 developmentally appropriate, picture-based, auto-gradable skills** spanning 25 topics across K-12 Computer Science Standards. All skills follow the established framework: minimal text, audio-supported, accessible (WCAG 2.1 AA), and designed for 2-5 minute completion times.

### Coverage Philosophy

- **Full Coverage (12 skills):** Core K-2 appropriate topics with comprehensive progression
- **Partial Coverage (2-11 skills):** Topics introducing concepts appropriate for K-2 readiness
- **Pre-Skills/Bridge (1-4 skills):** Grade 2 only, preparing for G3+ coding topics
- **Deferred to G3+ (11 topics):** Topics requiring coding, advanced concepts, or abstract thinking

- **1A-CS-01:** Selecting and operating appropriate software
- **1A-DA-05:** Storing, copying, searching, and retrieving information using technology
- **1A-DA-06:** Collecting and presenting data
- **1A-NI-04:** Modeling how information is broken down into smaller pieces and transmitted
- **1A-IC-16:** Comparing how people live and work with and without technology
- **1A-IC-17:** Working respectfully and responsibly with others online
- **1A-IC-18:** Keeping login information private and logging off devices
